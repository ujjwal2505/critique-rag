# Northwind Analytics — Product Overview

Northwind Analytics is a product analytics platform for web and backend
applications. It was first released in March 2021 and is operated by Northwind
Software, Inc., headquartered in Lisbon, Portugal.

The platform ingests events through a single REST endpoint and a set of
server-side SDKs. Customers use it to build funnels, retention curves, and
cohort reports without writing SQL.

Northwind Analytics is sold only as a hosted SaaS product. There is no
self-hosted or on-premises edition. All data is processed in the European Union
(Frankfurt region) regardless of where the customer is located.

The three product plans are Starter, Pro, and Enterprise. Feature and limit
differences between the plans are described in the pricing, data retention, and
support documents.
