# Northwind Analytics — Pricing and Plans

Northwind Analytics has three plans, billed monthly in euros.

## Starter
- Price: €0 per month (free).
- Included events: up to 100,000 tracked events per month.
- Seats: up to 3 team members.
- Overage: not available — once the monthly event cap is reached, further events
  are dropped until the next billing cycle.

## Pro
- Price: €290 per month.
- Included events: up to 5,000,000 tracked events per month.
- Seats: up to 25 team members.
- Overage: €0.50 per additional 10,000 events.

## Enterprise
- Price: custom, contracted annually. Contact sales for a quote.
- Included events: negotiated volume, starting at 50,000,000 events per month.
- Seats: unlimited team members.
- Overage: negotiated per contract.

All paid plans are billed in advance. Annual prepayment on the Pro plan receives
a 15% discount. There is no per-seat charge on any plan; seats are capped by
plan tier as listed above.
