# Northwind Analytics — Support and SLA

## Support channels
- Starter: community forum only.
- Pro: email support, with a target first response within 1 business day.
- Enterprise: email and a dedicated Slack channel, with a target first response
  within 2 hours during business hours.

Business hours are 09:00–18:00 Western European Time (WET), Monday to Friday,
excluding Portuguese public holidays.

## Uptime SLA
- Starter and Pro: no contractual uptime guarantee (best effort).
- Enterprise: 99.9% monthly uptime guarantee. If uptime falls below 99.9% in a
  calendar month, the customer receives service credits: 10% of the monthly fee
  for uptime between 99.0% and 99.9%, and 25% for uptime below 99.0%.

## Status page
A public status page reports current and historical platform availability.
Incidents are posted there within 30 minutes of detection.
